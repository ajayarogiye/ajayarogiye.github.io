About the Candidate

A Leader Shaped by
Service and Excellence

Ajaya Rogiye, DTM 2× is a dedicated Toastmasters leader currently serving as Division B Director in District 116. As a Double Distinguished Toastmaster (DTM 2×) — the highest recognition in Toastmasters International earned twice — she has demonstrated extraordinary mastery in both communication and leadership, fulfilling the most rigorous educational and service requirements the organisation offers.

Double Graduate (Commerce and Law) from Mumbai University, India. Ex-Banker, Educator and Trainer — Ajaya brings professional depth, analytical rigour, and a passion for developing people to every role she takes on.

With a consistent record of sponsoring new members — earning the 10-Member Sponsor Award twice and the 5-Member Sponsor Award three additional times — Ajaya has shown that she can deliver on the core mission of the organisation: growing and sustaining membership. Her five Triple Crown completions further reflect an extraordinary personal commitment to Pathways education.

Ajaya brings to the role of Program Quality Director a proven combination of educational rigour, member-focused leadership, and district-level operational experience that has been developed over many program years of active service.

Vision & Mission

Three Pillars for a
Stronger District 116

As Program Quality Director, the focus will be on raising educational standards, celebrating member achievements, and building a district culture where every Toastmaster feels supported and inspired to grow.

01

Elevate Education

Elevate Pathways education quality across every club — ensuring members complete their journeys with purpose, structure, and recognition through Pathways Mastery Workshops and a VPE Excellence Network.

  • Structured mentorship programs district-wide
  • More frequent educational workshops
  • Recognition systems that motivate growth
  • Dedicated support for VPEs and club officers
02

Unite Cultures

Build a district that celebrates diversity — bringing together members across languages and cultures through bilingual TLI Sessions and Multicultural Forums.

  • Bilingual TLI Sessions
  • Multicultural Forums
  • District-wide membership building campaigns
  • Retention initiatives for existing members
03

Empower Leaders

Cultivate the next generation of Toastmasters leaders through mentorship and a structured Leadership Progression Path at every level.

  • Club Officer Mentorship
  • Leadership Progression Path
  • Officer training enrichment workshops
  • Recognition of outstanding club leaders
